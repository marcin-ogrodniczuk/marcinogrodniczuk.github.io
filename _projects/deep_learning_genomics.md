Develop a deep learning model to predict three splicing events: splice junction presence, split site usage, and splice site classification, using
RNA-seq-derived labels. The model learns relationships between genomic DNA sequences and splicing patterns in human and mouse genomes to predict gene
expression behavior from sequence data.
